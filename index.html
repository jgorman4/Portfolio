Hello there! I am Geospatial Specialist with the Texas A&M Forest Service. I have developed a passion for solving complex natural resource issues with various geospatial plaforms and tools, especially as it pertains to wildlife conservation. This passion grew from my combined experience as an Imagery Intelligence Anaylst for the Army National Guard, a Rangeland Conservationist for the USDA-NRCS, and from undergraduate and graduate degrees. To further my interest in GIS, I pursued a second Master of Science in Geographic Information Science. As the world of GIS technology rapidly evolves, I love to learn new skill sets that help us solve our world's most pressing issues. I am always looking for opportunities to improve my programming knowledge with Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and SQL, imagery analysis with Google Earth Engine, and deep learning with AI and Machine Learning.
